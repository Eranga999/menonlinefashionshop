# Fashion Shopping App

The **Fashion Shopping App** is designed to help shoppers unleash their full potential by allowing them to browse various fashion items, add them to their cart, and complete their shopping experience with a seamless checkout process. The app offers a curated collection of fashion stores specifically for men, making it easier for them to find and buy clothing items from one convenient platform. Users can also maintain and update their profiles, ensuring they have a personalized shopping experience.

### **Features**

- **Browse Fashion Items:** Shoppers can browse through a variety of fashion items and choose their preferred items.
- **Add to Cart:** Users can add items to their cart and manage their shopping list.
- **Checkout and Payments:** After shopping, users can proceed to checkout and make payments to complete their orders.
- **Profile Management:** Users can maintain and update their personal profile, ensuring a customized shopping experience.
- **Minimalistic Design:** The app uses a white background to ensure that users can easily view prices and clothing colors without any distractions.
- **Color Scheme:** Proper use of colors throughout the app ensures a visually appealing and user-friendly design.
