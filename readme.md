The below application was designed to allow shoppers to unleash their full potential allowing them to shop through and add various items to their cart. There are few fashion stores for men, so I arranged this to fulfill their needs in one place. After satisfying their shopping needs, they can finally check out after making their payments. They also can maintain and update their respective profile in this application, a white color is added to the background so that the user can see the price and clothes colors without interruption. Also, the color rule is used properly to complete the final product.
